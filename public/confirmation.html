Your booking is confirmed and we are looking forward to meeting you! All the details below will be emailed to you but please read carefully.

Things to remember

Your lesson will start on the dot of your time slot. If you can, please try to arrive at least 5 minutes early, so you can get in to the studio and be ready to start on time.

If you are late - CALL me on my phone and I can let you in (don’t text as I won’t notice it)

Please try to remember to bring your own hand towel. You’ll need it to wipe your sweat away and to clean your pole during your session. You will 100% need a cloth, so try to remember one.

What to wear

T shirt and shorts - Please bring leggings to warm up and cool down in - We often learn slide moves on the floor, so bring socks , even if it's hot. A vest top is better as some upper arm skin is really useful to grip.

Re the shorts, they must be short enough for your thighs to be exposed or shorts that you can easily roll up when you need more thigh exposed.

For Foot wear we dance in bare feet.

Make sure you take off all your rings, brackets and watches and take out belly button rings before the lesson.

Health & skin preparation

We will ask you at the start of every class if you have any injuries or illnesses that we need to know about... However please let me know ASAP if you have any health issues AT ALL- This could include, pregnancy, post pregnancy, dizziness, epilepsy, any aches and pains- previous broken bones/ ligament damage etc... Pole is a low impact sport, so its a safe form of exercise for most people but its vital to discuss any health issues before starting the course.

Make sure you do NOT apply ANY body lotion or moisturiser on your legs, arms or hands (Not even a tiny bit ) on the day if the lesson at ALL ...Even the tiniest bit of grease; on any part of your body will then transfer on to the pole and will cause other body parts to slip …This will make moves almost impossible and very dangerous as your grip will be compromised. All of your body will be touching the pole as some point in the lesson. Except for your face.

Cancelations & class details

If you do need to cancel your class, unfortunately your missed lesson IS NOT REFUNDABLE and we cannot carry your missed lesson over to a new course.
However if you know you have a holiday booked and you let me know before the start of the course. We will try to find you a slot at the Brockwell Lido in Herne Hill on a Monday evening.
This means you would come to two lessons in one week- to make up for your missed class.
NB- this is only possible if we have availability in another venue that suits your level and please be aware that your missed class, can’t be carried over to a new course- you must make it up in the same term.

The only exception to this is… Total Beginners - We can't make up missed lessons to Total Beginners as we often only have one total beginners course running, so we have no where for you to swap to.

If you plan on missing more than one week of a course - you can choose PAYG classes at £25 per class.

Any questions about all the above let me know.

After a 5 week course, we usually have a one week break in between courses.
