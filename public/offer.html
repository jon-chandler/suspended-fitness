What we offer

Formally Pole Precision, we’ve been teaching South East London pole dancing or pole fitness since 2004.

As teachers, we aren't tiny ex gymnasts or ex pro dancers. We are normal people who found pole and aerial and want to share this fantastic sport and art form to everyone, because if we can do it, so can you.

Our teachers are safest, DBS checked, ETM qualified, pole and aerial qualified and fully Insured.
Check our google reviews for what our students say about us and them book on to one of our courses, you won’t regret it.
