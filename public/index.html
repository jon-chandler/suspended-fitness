Suspended Fitness

Strength and confidence through pole and aerial - no experience needed.

We've been teaching pole dance fitness for over 20 years, which means we've learnt exactly how to get the best out of you, so you will gain a huge amount of strength and technique in a relaxed and safe environment.

What We Offer

  • Total Beginners Pole Course
  • Improver Course
  • Intermediate Course
  • Upper Inter / Advanced Course
  • Aerial Hammock
  • Kids and Teens
  • Online Flexibility Courses

About Us

Our specialist courses have been exclusively created by us, so you'll achieved far more than our competitors in our down to earth, low key style.

We're proud to say that our online flexibility classes, have got a ton of our students in to the splits. And, we're now sharing our pole and fitness knowledge to the next generation, with our teen pole classes.

Next in the offering will be aerial hammock classes, which will be a great addition to suspended fitness.

Testimonials

We've said this a lot over the years. We are everyday people who found pole and aerial and want to share this fantastic sport and art form to everyone, because if we can do it, so can you.

You'll be blown away with what you can achieve with us in such a short amount of time. Whether you are 14 or 64. Whether you’re a Total Beginner or an Advanced student.
